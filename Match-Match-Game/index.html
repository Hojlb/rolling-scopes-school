This Is the Match-Match Game!

How To Start

Select a card style and a difficulty level and press "Start Game" button.

Rules

Find all the pairs! Click on a card to reveal the number on the other side. Click on another card and try to find a match to the first. If you succeed, the pair will be removed from the game. If not, try again!

How To Win

You will be a winner when all the pairs are found.
